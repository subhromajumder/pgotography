About Me

Hello I am Ashwini Suresh a full time independent photographer currently based in Bangalore and Hyderabad. To me photography is a beautiful way to capture life and moments. I see myself as a picture poet who hanker for photographs and stories. I specialise in portraits. Open to new commission and collaboration. Please email me.
